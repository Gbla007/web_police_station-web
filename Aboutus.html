About Us

The police department is dedicated to serving and protecting the community.
We are committed to providing professional and efficient services to ensure the safety and well-being of all citizens.
Our mission is to work in partnership with the community to solve problems, prevent crime and enhance the quality of life in our city.

Our Values

  • Integrity
  • Service
  • Fairness
  • Respect
  • Accountability

Our History

The police department was established in 1900 and has been serving the community for over 120 years. Over the years, we have grown and evolved to meet the changing needs of our city and its residents. Our officers receive ongoing training and use the latest technology and techniques to ensure the highest level of service to the community.

FAQ

What is the non-emergency number to reach the police?

The non-emergency number to reach the police is .

076-56-70-69

What should I do in case of an emergency?

In case of an emergency, please call 091 immediately.

How do I file a police report?

You can file a police report by calling the non-emergency number, visiting the police station in person, or online through the police department's website.

What are the visiting hours for the police station?

The visiting hours for the police station are Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.

How can I obtain a copy of a police report?

You can obtain a copy of a police report by visiting the police station in person, submitting a request through the mail, or online through the police department's website.

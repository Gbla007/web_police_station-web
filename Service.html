Our Services

Patrol

Our patrol division is responsible for maintaining public safety and responding to emergencies in a timely manner. Officers are on patrol 24/7 to ensure the safety and security of our community.

Investigation

Our investigation division is dedicated to solving crimes and bringing those responsible to justice. Our detectives work tirelessly to gather evidence, interview witnesses, and build strong cases.

Traffic Enforcement

Our traffic enforcement division is responsible for promoting safe driving and reducing traffic accidents in our community. Officers enforce traffic laws and educate drivers on safe driving practices.

Emergency Response

Our emergency response division is trained and equipped to handle a wide range of emergencies, from natural disasters to active shooter situations. Our officers are dedicated to protecting and serving our community in times of crisis.
